About Me

I am a passionate and easy-going individual born and raised in South Auckland, New Zealand.
Whilst living at home with my wonderful partner and 2 energetic dogs, I have just completed a Bachelors of Digital Technologies (Majoring in Software Development). IT and Computing has always held a facination for me, but recently I have been focusing on Web Development and have formed a major passion and interest for front-end development.

When I am not working on projects or spending time with my family, I can be caught gaming, excersing and hanging out with good friends. Travel and nature is another passion of mine, as I yearn to visit other countries around the globe in the future.
